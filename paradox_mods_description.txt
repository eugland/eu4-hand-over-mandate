Description:

HAND OVER THE MANDATE

The Son of Heaven may now abdicate with grace. As Emperor of China, offer the Mandate of Heaven to a nation of your choosing — any culture, any religion — instead of losing it in fire and rebellion.

FEATURES

• Abdication decision — pick from the 8 strongest eligible nations (exact ranking by total development). Candidates: anyone bordering you or any of your tributaries.
• They may refuse — the chosen nation receives the offer and can decline. Refusers are excluded when you pick again.
• Clean handover — no "Mandate of Heaven lost" malus, no stability hit; the abdicating emperor even gains prestige.
• 5-year cooldown — the Mandate cannot ping-pong between friends.
• AI emperors abdicate too — a collapsing AI dynasty (Mandate below 20) passes the torch to the strongest candidate.
• Diplomatic fallout — the new Emperor is grateful (+50); passed-over candidates resent them (−25); refusing the offer slights the old Emperor.
• Tributaries may follow the Mandate — choose whether your tributaries transfer to the new Son of Heaven.
• Opened Take Mandate CB — religion and culture gates removed from the vanilla casus belli: any nation bordering the Emperor can go to war for the Mandate.

REQUIREMENTS

• EU4 1.37.x
• Mandate of Heaven DLC

COMPATIBILITY

Everything is additive except one file: the mod overrides the vanilla cb_take_mandate, so it conflicts only with mods that also change that casus belli. Works mid-save. Localised in English, French, German and Spanish (English text).

HOW IT WORKS

Decision "Hand Over the Mandate" (visible only as Emperor of China, at peace) → event lists the top 8 candidates → your chosen nation accepts or refuses → on acceptance the Mandate, and optionally your tributaries, pass to the new Son of Heaven.

Ironman and achievements are disabled, as with any mod.
